How Many Leaflets Do You Need in London?

One of the most common questions businesses ask is how many leaflets they need for a successful campaign in London. The answer depends on your target area, business type and campaign goals.

Small test campaigns

A smaller campaign might start with around 5,000 leaflets. This can work well for testing a new offer or targeting a specific neighbourhood such as Camden or Islington.

Standard local campaigns

Many London businesses start with 10,000 to 20,000 leaflets. This gives enough coverage to create repeated visibility and improve the chances of getting enquiries.

Larger area coverage

If you want to cover bigger locations such as Croydon, Stratford or Hackney, you may need 20,000+ leaflets depending on postcode density.

Why quantity matters

Leaflet distribution works best when enough households see your brand. A campaign that is too small may not generate enough awareness, while a larger and better-targeted campaign often performs more strongly.

Plan around your budget

The best approach is to match leaflet quantity with your budget and area size. Using a quote calculator helps you work out the right campaign size before launching.

Conclusion

The number of leaflets you need in London depends on where you want to target and how much visibility you want to create. Starting with a realistic campaign size gives you a better chance of generating consistent local leads.
